    Summary of Appendix F: Recreational Services
    Appendix F outlines the requirements for Recreational Services at Al Dhafra Air Base, encompassing the Community Recreational Program (CRP) and Fitness Program to support morale, welfare, and recreation for approximately 1,600 daily customers (up to 4,000 during surges). Services include program planning, execution, labor, and supervision across facilities such as the Community Activity Center (C.A.C.), Thirsty Camel bar, Phantom Auditorium, Pavilion, fitness centers, satellite gyms, and outdoor sports areas. Operations are generally 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with tasks involving customer service, resale/alcohol activities, events, maintenance, inventories, and reporting. Local Nationals (LN) and Other Country Nationals (OCN) are authorized for supervised tasks but not cash handling.
    Workload Drivers by Section

        Support for 1,600 daily customers across CRP and Fitness Program facilities.
        24/7 coverage unless otherwise specified in subsections.
        Authorization for LN/OCN personnel under US National (USN) supervision for non-cash tasks.
