SOW–Deliverable Linking (Compact)

Goal:
- Connect work statements to deliverables and CDRLs.

Rules:
- Identify tasks/objectives/metrics in SOW/PWS; find deliverables in Section J or embedded in SOW.
- Link STATEMENT_OF_WORK → DELIVERABLE via CONTAINS/APPLIES_TO; align due dates with submission_instruction.

Output:
- Entities: statement_of_work, deliverable, submission_instruction.
- Relationships: CONTAINS, APPLIES_TO, GUIDES.
