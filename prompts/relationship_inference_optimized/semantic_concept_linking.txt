Semantic Concept Linking (Compact)

Goal:
- Connect abstract concepts (readiness, risk, interoperability) to concrete entities when explicit references exist.

Rules:
- Link CONCEPT → REQUIREMENT/EVALUATION_FACTOR when text ties the idea to obligations or evaluation.
- Avoid speculative links; require explicit phrases or repeated emphasis.

Output:
- Entities: concept, requirement, evaluation_factor.
- Relationships: RELATED_TO.
