Instruction–Evaluation Linking (Compact)

Goal:
- Link embedded submission instructions to the evaluation factors they guide.

Rules:
- Detect page limits, format/margins, volume structure, deadlines near factor language.
- Create SUBMISSION_INSTRUCTION and link via EVALUATED_BY to the nearest relevant evaluation_factor.
- Maintain volume/factor mapping and importance context.

Output:
- Entities: submission_instruction, evaluation_factor.
- Relationships: EVALUATED_BY.
