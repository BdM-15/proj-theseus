Deliverable Traceability (Compact)

Goal:
- Ensure deliverables (CDRLs) trace to SOW tasks and due dates.

Rules:
- Identify deliverables via CDRL refs (A001, DD Form 1423), report names.
- Link STATEMENT_OF_WORK tasks → DELIVERABLE via APPLIES_TO/GUIDES.
- Capture due_date, format, frequency; validate against submission_instruction.
- Create program-level summary of deliverables.

Output:
- Entities: deliverable, statement_of_work, submission_instruction.
- Relationships: APPLIES_TO/GUIDES links; CONTAINS where listed in Section J.
