Clause Clustering (Compact)

Goal:
- Organize clauses by regulation/supplement and theme; attribute correctly.

Rules:
- Detect patterns: FAR 52.###-##, DFARS 252.###-####, AFFARS 5352.###-##, NMCARS 5252.###-####, plus HSAR/DOSAR/GSAM/VAAR/DEAR/NFS/AIDAR/CAR/DIAR/DOLAR/EDAR/EPAAR/FEHBAR/HHSAR/HUDAR/IAAR/JAR/LIFAR/NRCAR/SOFARS/TAR.
- Extract supplement from number; group similar clauses (supplies/services, delivery, security).
- Attribute to Section I (contract clauses); reps/certs to Section K.

Output:
- Entities: clause (clause_number, regulation, date).
- Relationships: APPLIES_TO → program/requirement; ATTRIBUTED_TO → section.
