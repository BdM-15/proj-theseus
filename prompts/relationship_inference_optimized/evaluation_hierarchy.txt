Evaluation Hierarchy (Compact)

Goal:
- Preserve factor hierarchy and importance/weights.

Rules:
- Factor → Subfactor → Sub-subfactor (CHILD_OF relationships).
- Carry attributes: importance (Most/Significantly More), weights (points/%), ratings (Excellent/Good/etc.).
- Map non-standard labels (Selection Criteria) to evaluation_factor structure.
- Link embedded submission instructions: SUBMISSION_INSTRUCTION --EVALUATED_BY--> EVALUATION_FACTOR.

Output:
- Entities: evaluation_factor (with importance/weights/subfactors).
- Relationships: CHILD_OF links for hierarchy; EVALUATED_BY links for instructions.
