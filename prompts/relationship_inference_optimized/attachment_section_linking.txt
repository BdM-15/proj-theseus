Attachment–Section Linking (Compact)

Goal:
- Link attachments/annex/exhibits to parent sections and referenced content.

Rules:
- Prefix patterns (J-####, Attachment #, Annex ##) → parent Section J.
- Link SECTION --CONTAINS--> DOCUMENT; DOCUMENT --CONTAINS--> STATEMENT_OF_WORK or specifications.
- Map references in text ("See Attachment X") via REFERENCES.

Output:
- Entities: section, document, statement_of_work.
- Relationships: CONTAINS, REFERENCES.
